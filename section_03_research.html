Competitive Analysis Zocdoc · Practo · HealthTap
Key Findings
F01

Trust is the #1 blocker. Users spent an average of 4.2 minutes on a doctor's profile before deciding — and 7 of 11 still left without booking, citing "not enough to go on." The most common missing signal: how long the doctor had been practicing.

F02

Availability uncertainty causes anxiety. When users couldn't see real-time slots, they assumed the doctor was unavailable and exited — even when slots existed.

F03

Post-visit silence breaks loyalty. Users who couldn't follow up in-app churned within 30 days. Those with in-app messaging returned 3× more often.

F04

Booking flows have too many steps. The average competing app required 7+ taps to confirm a booking. Users expected to be done in 3.
