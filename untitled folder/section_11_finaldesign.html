04 — Final Design
Refined after testing
Filter visibility
Filter chips elevated with section label
Added "Filter by" heading above chips with 12px separation from search bar. 5/6 users who missed filters in round 1 found them immediately in round 2.
Slot selection
2-step indicator added to booking flow
Explicit "Select date → Pick time" stepper added. Task completion for slot selection jumped from 33% to 100% between test rounds.
Payment transparency
Total shown before method selection
Consultation fee now displayed prominently in the booking summary card, anchored above payment method selection. Eliminated all hesitation drop-offs.
