Emily prioritises her health but has almost no time to manage it. She expects her healthcare apps to be as intuitive as her banking and food delivery apps — fast, clear, and stress-free.

Needs
Find a specialist quickly without endless scrolling
See real availability before tapping into a profile
Book, reschedule, and cancel with minimal friction
Stay connected with her doctor after the visit

Confusing multi-step forms that ask the same thing twice

Apps that go silent after the appointment ends

Jobs to be Done
When I need a doctor Help me find the right one fast, without second-guessing my choice.
When I book an appointment Make it feel as easy as ordering food — 3 taps, done.
After my visit Let me follow up without leaving the app.
Why Emily represents the core segment — She sits at the intersection of high digital literacy and high healthcare anxiety. She's not looking for a medical encyclopedia — she wants a product that respects her time, earns her trust, and stays useful after the appointment. Designing for Emily means designing for the majority of our active users.
