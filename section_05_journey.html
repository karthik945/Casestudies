⚡ Surprising

Booking wasn't the hard part — choosing was. Users described spending 15–20 mins just deciding on a doctor, then booking in under 2 minutes. The friction wasn't the form. It was the decision before it.

↓ Biggest gap

After booking, users felt abandoned. In interviews, every participant described the days between booking and appointment as "silence." No prep, no way to ask a question, no reminder that it was real. One user: "I screenshot the confirmation because I didn't trust the app would remember."

→ What this shaped

Two design priorities emerged from the map: reduce decision friction on the doctor selection screen, and keep the care loop alive between booking and visit — not just during it.
