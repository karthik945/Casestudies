Dark-first, high contrast
Deep navy backgrounds reduce eye strain. Blue-purple gradients signal trust, not urgency.
02
Colour as status
Blue = action, green = success, amber = alerts. Users understand state without reading labels.
03
44px minimum touch targets
Every button, slot, and chip meets WCAG 2.1 AA tap target requirements — no accidental taps.
04
One primary CTA per screen
Each screen has exactly one gradient button. The next step is always clear — no competing actions.
