01
User Problem

Finding the right doctor feels like a guessing game

People searching for a specialist have no reliable way to know if a doctor is actually right for their situation. Generic listings show name and degree — nothing about wait times, whether they take new patients, or when they're actually available. The result: users open multiple apps, make phone calls, and still aren't sure they made the right choice.

Backed by: Accenture Digital Health Consumer Survey 2023 — 29% of patients have switched providers due to poor digital experience
01
The Solution

Structured filtering with live availability

A smart search layer with specialty, location, language, and rating filters — combined with real-time slot visibility. Users see exactly when a doctor is free before tapping into their profile.

02
User Problem

Thin profiles make it impossible to trust a stranger with your health

A doctor's name, photo, and a one-line bio is not enough for someone to feel confident handing over their health. Yet that's the standard on most platforms. Users want to know: does this doctor take my condition seriously? Will they actually respond? Do other patients feel heard? Without those signals, the default response is doubt — and doubt means abandonment.

Backed by: PwC Health Research Institute — 75% of patients say trust in their provider's digital tools directly affects their trust in the provider
02
The Solution

Rich doctor profiles that build trust

Detailed profiles surface verified credentials, patient reviews, video introductions, and specialty highlights. Trust scores and badge systems let users make confident decisions faster.

03
User Problem

Booking a doctor takes longer than it should

Most healthcare apps treat booking like a form submission — specialty, insurance, date, reason for visit, emergency contact… by step 6, users have given up. There's no visibility into real availability until deep into the flow, no sense of progress, and no reassurance that the appointment is actually confirmed until an email arrives hours later.

Backed by: KLAS Research 2023 — appointment scheduling friction is the #1 cited reason for patient digital disengagement
03
The Solution

3-step booking with clear progress states

A streamlined booking flow: Pick slot → Confirm details → Done. Progress indicators, animated confirmations, and instant calendar sync make the experience feel fast and certain.

04
User Problem

Once the appointment ends, the app becomes useless

Healthcare doesn't end when you walk out of the clinic. Follow-up questions come up. Prescriptions need clarifying. Results arrive with no context. But the app that helped you book the appointment has nothing to offer after it ends — leaving patients to text their doctor on WhatsApp, call the front desk, or just sit with their uncertainty.

Backed by: Rock Health 2023 Digital Health Consumer Adoption Report — post-visit engagement is the lowest-rated feature across all consumer health apps
04
The Solution

In-app messaging and visit history

A post-visit hub with secure doctor messaging, visit summaries, prescription history, and smart follow-up reminders — keeping the care relationship alive long after the appointment ends.
