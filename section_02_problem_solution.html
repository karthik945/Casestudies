Solution

02
01
User Problem

No clear way to find the right doctor

Users are overwhelmed by unfiltered doctor lists with no specialty sorting, no real availability shown, and no way to compare profiles meaningfully. They resort to phone calls or give up entirely.

01
The Solution

Structured filtering with live availability

A smart search layer with specialty, location, language, and rating filters — combined with real-time slot visibility. Users see exactly when a doctor is free before tapping into their profile.

02
User Problem

Users don't trust doctors they haven't met

Thin doctor profiles with stock photos and generic bios fail to build confidence. Users struggle with deciding — browsing too many options and frequently abandoning the booking entirely.

02
The Solution

Rich doctor profiles that build trust

Detailed profiles surface verified credentials, patient reviews, video introductions, and specialty highlights. Trust scores and badge systems let users make confident decisions faster.

03
User Problem

Booking flow is multi-step and confusing

Existing apps require too many taps, ask for redundant information, and give no clarity on booking status. Users frequently hit dead ends with no available slots and no guidance on what to do next.

03
The Solution

3-step booking with clear progress states

A streamlined booking flow: Pick slot → Confirm details → Done. Progress indicators, animated confirmations, and instant calendar sync make the experience feel fast and certain.

04
User Problem

No communication after the appointment

Once the visit ends, the app goes silent. Users have no way to follow up with their doctor, access visit notes, or manage ongoing care — forcing them off-platform to text, email, or call.

04
The Solution

In-app messaging and visit history

A post-visit hub with secure doctor messaging, visit summaries, prescription history, and smart follow-up reminders — keeping the care relationship alive long after the appointment ends.
